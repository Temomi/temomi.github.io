Pan

Pan is a Croatian lager beer, which is produced by Carlsberg Croatia. Top Croatian lager Pan appeared on the market in 1997 and from the very beginning emphasized refreshing beer as an important feature. With an alcohol content of 4.8%, Pan its smoothness and high quality liquid brings refreshment for any gathering. With less bitterness, but still with a full taste, it’s the best choice if you want to relax or just quench your thirst. Refresh yourself and enjoy.
