Osječko

A high quality pale lager beer from the first Croatain brewery with natural ingredients and water from our own wells. A brewing tradition in Osijek, over three centuries long, is dedicated to the production of the original Osječko pivo (beer from Osijek). The water from our own springs, the highest-quality barley, corn and hops and painstaking care, experience and the right dosage of Slavonian pride create the unique ingredients that, combined together, make this relaxing and refreshing beer. ABV dropped from 5% do 4.5% in 2010. Old comm. description: A lager beer with 12% extract: a traditional beer with a flavour characteristic for a German lager and wheat beer. It has 5% alcohol by volume.
